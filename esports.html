RAINBOW SIX WORLD CUP

The Rainbow Six World Cup is an exciting new way to enjoy Rainbow Six Esports. It is the latest addition in the Rainbow Six esports ecosystem; a new international competition in which 20 countries and regions will compete in the summer of 2021 in hopes of being crowned World Champions. For fans, it will be the opportunity to share their love, pride and unconditional support with the team that will represent them on the worldwide stage.
